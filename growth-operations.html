About The Role

We're looking for a Growth Operations Intern to support the systems, experiments, and process discipline behind scalable growth execution. You'll help connect data, workflows, and campaign operations so the team can test faster and learn more efficiently.

What You'll Do

  • Support campaign operations, tracking hygiene, reporting workflows, and experiment coordination.
  • Help maintain structured growth processes across channels, assets, and performance reviews.
  • Use AI tools to accelerate research, analysis, documentation, and execution support for growth initiatives.
  • Synthesize experiment results into clear summaries, learnings, and recommended next steps.
  • Contribute to playbooks and documentation so repeatable growth workflows can be standardized.

What We Look For

  • Currently pursuing or recently completed a relevant degree, ideally from leading universities such as NUS, NTU, and SMU. Strong candidates from other institutions will also be considered.
  • Strong academic performance, ideally distinction or above, alongside evidence of initiative, ownership, and high-quality execution.
  • Comfortable using AI tools beyond simple chat workflows to accelerate analysis, drafting, research, and execution.
  • High attention to detail and discipline in checking facts, requirements, and deliverables before sharing work.
  • Able to thrive in a fast-moving startup environment with ambiguity, rapid iteration, and cross-functional collaboration.
  • Strong interest in growth systems, experimentation, analytics, and process quality.
  • Comfort working with data, spreadsheets, operational detail, and rapid iteration.

Why Apply

This is a front-row seat to venture building. You'll work on real startup-creation problems, learn how ideas are validated and operationalized, and build judgment across strategy, product, growth, and execution.
