Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Horizon Labs handles the information you share with us through this website.

What We Collect

When you apply for a role or send a pitch, we collect the details you choose to provide, such as your name, email address, links, and message. We do not collect more than you give us.

How We Use It

We use your information solely to review your application or pitch and to contact you about it. We do not sell your information.

Retention

We keep application information for as long as needed to consider you for current or future roles, after which it is removed on request.

Your Choices

You can ask us to access or delete the information you have shared by emailing admin@horizonlabs.ai.
